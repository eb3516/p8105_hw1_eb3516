The dataset early_january_weather has variables origin, year, month,
day, hour, temp, dewp, humid, win_dir, wind_speed, wind_gust, precip,
pressure, visib, and time_hour. All the data has origin EWR and measures
hourly weather factors from January 1 - Jan 15, 2013.

According to nrow there are 358 rows and according to ncol there are 15
columns.
